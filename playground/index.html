Welcome to the aXe Playground. Do you wanna know what aXe can do for you? Give it a try! Use the example we put in or press "CLEAR FIELD" and put in your own piece of code. In "SELECT AN EXAMPLE" you can set a specific rule you want aXe to check. If you're ready to go press "ANALYSE HTML".

What do you want aXe to check for you? Select Rule or Check and choose your specific preset. If you are done, press "APPLY SELECTED PRESET".
